Privacy Policy

Draft — takes effect at commercial launch · written 21 July 2026
The short version: your learning data lives on your device, your spoken audio is never sent to our servers, and we don't sell or share anything with advertisers.

Isshin ("the app", "we") is a Japanese speaking-practice app. This policy explains, in plain language, what information the app handles and where it goes.

What we collect and why

1. Your learning progress — stays on your device

Words you've studied, drill history, streaks, settings, and conversation recaps are stored locally on your phone. Once accounts launch, this progress can also sync to your account so you don't lose it when you change phones. It is never used for anything except showing you your own progress.

2. Your voice — processed by your device, not by us

When you speak, speech-to-text is performed by your device's built-in speech service (for example, Google's speech recognition on Android, or Apple's on iOS). Your audio goes from your device to that service under their privacy terms — it is never sent to, stored on, or heard by Isshin's servers. Isshin only receives the resulting text transcript, on your device, to drive the conversation.

3. Conversation text — sent to an AI provider to generate replies

The live conversation feature works by sending the text of the conversation (your transcribed words and the partner's previous replies) to an AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the partner's next reply. This text is sent over an encrypted connection. We send only what is needed for the conversation to work — never your name, email, or account details alongside it. Per Anthropic's API terms, API inputs are not used to train their models.

4. Account details — when you sign in

When accounts launch, signing in with Google or Apple gives us your email address and a display name. We use these only to: identify your subscription, sync your progress, and contact you about your account. We never sell them, share them with advertisers, or use them for marketing you didn't ask for.

5. Payments — handled by the app stores

Subscriptions are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We never see or store your card number. We receive only a confirmation that your subscription is active.

What we do NOT do

  • No selling of data. Ever, to anyone.
  • No advertising trackers. The app contains no ad networks and no third-party analytics trackers.
  • No audio storage. Your voice recordings are not kept by us — anywhere.
  • No training on your conversations. Neither Isshin nor (per their API terms) our AI provider trains models on your conversation content.

Data retention and deletion

Learning data on your device is yours: uninstalling the app (or clearing site data) removes it. Once accounts launch, you can delete your account and its synced data from the app's Settings, or by emailing us — we will delete synced progress and account details within 30 days.

Children

Isshin is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under the Australian Privacy Principles, GDPR, and CCPA), you have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal information we hold about you. Email us and we'll do it — no forms, no runaround.
