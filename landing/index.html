Questions

I can't read Japanese at all. Can I start?

Yes — Isshin starts from zero. Everything is written in kana (the phonetic script) with audio on every word, and the app teaches you kana itself. No kanji is thrown at you.

Is my voice recorded or stored?

No. Speech recognition happens through your phone's built-in service; Isshin's servers never receive, store, or hear your audio. Details in our privacy policy.

What phones does it work on?

The free preview runs in the browser on Android (Chrome) today, with iPhone app support planned for launch. The conversation features need a device with speech recognition — most modern phones have it.

Is it really judgment-free?

Literally. The app contains no grades, scores, red X's, or "wrong!" anywhere — that's a hard design rule, not marketing. Your partner responds the way a patient friend would.
