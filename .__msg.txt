v7.43 — inline conjugation strip on Focus word cards

Each Focus word card now shows its everyday variations right where you
study it, using the existing conjugation engine:
- verbs: negative (toNai), past plain/polite (toTa / toMashita),
  past-negative (toNakatta), te-form (toTe)
- i-adjectives: negative (toKunai), past (toKatta), past-negative

Tap any form to hear it (speakJP). Kana only. Renders via focusFormsStrip(w)
between the nuance note and the example sentences; non-conjugable parts of
speech show nothing.
