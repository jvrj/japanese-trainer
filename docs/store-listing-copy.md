## Long description (draft)

**Isshin is a Japanese conversation partner, not a quiz.**

Most apps make you tap flashcards for months before you ever speak. Isshin
starts with your voice: within 90 seconds of opening the app you'll have your
first short spoken exchange — no signup, no score, no judgment.

- 🗣 **Real conversations, hands-free.** Talk with a warm AI partner who
  listens patiently, follows wherever you take the conversation, and shares
  its own answers like a friend would — 14 everyday topics or free talk.
- 💬 **Corrections that don't sting.** When there's a more natural way to say
  something, your partner simply says it back naturally inside the
  conversation — an observation, never a red X. Research on conversational
  practice backs this: a 2023 review of 32 studies (Jeon) found
  conversation-partner practice is one of the most effective ways to build
  real speaking ability.
- 🎌 **Japanese only, done deeply.** Not 40 languages wide — one language
  deep: kana-first reading, pitch-pattern-aware audio, politeness and nuance
  notes on every word, and a vocabulary that grows with what you actually say.
- 🔁 **Drills that feed the conversation.** Hands-free audio-led vocab drills
  (use them while driving), verb-form ladders, and spaced repetition — all
  pointed at one goal: holding a real conversation.
- 📈 **Progress you can feel.** No grades, no leaderboards, no guilt streaks —
  Isshin measures minutes talked and words spoken out loud, and plays your own
  Japanese sentences back to you at the end of every conversation.

Whether you're starting from zero or un-freezing years of textbook Japanese,
Isshin gives you a place to speak badly, safely — which is how speaking well
actually starts.
